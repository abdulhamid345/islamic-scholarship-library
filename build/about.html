About the Yahaya Bawa Islamic Library

Preserving and sharing the timeless works of Islamic scholars

Our Mission

To provide easy access to the scholarly works of the Sokoto Caliphate and beyond, ensuring these invaluable resources are preserved and shared with the global community.

Our Vision

To inspire future generations through the preservation of Islamic knowledge, fostering a deep connection with our rich scholarly heritage.

Our History

Foundation

Named after the respected scholar Yahaya Bawa, our library was established to preserve the intellectual legacy of the Sokoto Caliphate.

Early Collections

The library began collecting manuscripts and works from Sheik Abdullahi Bn Fodio and Sheik Muhammad Bello, establishing a foundation of scholarly excellence.

Digital Transformation

Embracing modern technology to make our collections accessible to scholars and students worldwide.
